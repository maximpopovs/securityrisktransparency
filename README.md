**Cloud Security Posture & Risk Dashboard Project**

### 2. Business Problem
Modern cloud environments generate large volumes of vulnerabilities, identity alerts, and incident data.  
However, organizations often lack **centralized, measurable visibility**, leading to:

- Fragmented reporting across tools and teams  
- No trend analysis or remediation accountability  
- Leadership decisions made without metrics  
- Reactive security posture instead of proactive risk reduction  

This increases risk exposure and slows remediation.

---

### 3. Objective
Build a unified security posture program that aggregates vulnerability, identity, and incident data into a Power BI dashboard, enabling:

- Data-driven risk prioritization  
- Executive visibility and accountability  
- Measurable improvements in remediation  
- Continuous reduction of high-risk exposure
